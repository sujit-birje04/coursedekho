About

India holds an important place in the global education industry. The country has more than 1.4 million schools with over 227 million students enrolled and more than 36,000 higher education institutes. India has one of the largest higher education systems in the world. However, there is still a lot of potential for further development in the education system.

Multiple institutes offering multiple skill set are initiating more and more every year making one of the biggest market in India. CoursesDekho.com is developed with an intention to bridge the gap between student and institute offering required skills. CoursesDekho.com is a platform where students can locate all the information of a specific training institute they need to find and compare the details like faculties, syllabus and duration along with the fees for the same provided by training institutes of multiple domains and decide the best one suitable for them.

Through CoursesDekho.com a user can browse through all the listed training institute from any convenient location. It also provides all the listed training institute a platform where they can provide all the required details to get a better exposure in the field which helps them to reach out to more users through

Key Features

Compare your Institutes:

The compare page of the CoursesDekho.com lets you select top five institutes that matches yours requirement and view them at one place with the important information available with filters.

Book demo class:

Students can book/request a demo class from the training institute that they are interested in joining to analyse the training experience of the institute.

Reviews:

The reviews visible of the institutes are only from the students who have completed the training from that institute which we track with our featured product, Buddy.
